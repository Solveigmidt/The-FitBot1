Feature Breakdown

Custom Workout Plans

FitBot customises workout plans suited for you, based on your goals, level and training needs.

Built-in Timer

The chest display keeps track of time and tells you exactly how many seconds you have left during each interval or break.

Music & Voice Control

The side speakers can play music, and you can communicate with FitBot to make it play whatever you want.

Customize Your FitBot

Customize it to the color that suits you. You want it to change to purple? Just say “FitBot, change color to purple”, and it will do exactly as you want.
